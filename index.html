How To Play

Get some teammates together and create your own FantasyXC league! This game is specificially to be played on a single device and there is no cross-device linking. The website will, however, save your teams once they have been drafted so you can go back and enter the results later.

  1. 1

    Create Teams

    Enter your FantasyXC team name and then your own name in the coach input. Add 2-4 teams in order to start the draft. You can remove a team by clicking the X in the corner.

  2. 2

    Select Runners

    Once you've created the teams, use the filters to choose who will be available for the draft. You can unselect individual runners by clicking on their name if a runner is not running for any reason. Runners are in ALL groups that apply to them so you may have to unselect them depending on the meet.

  3. 3

    Draft Runners

    Once you start the draft a team will be randomly selected to begin. The turns will follow a snake-draft order until each team has selected 7 runners. You can sort by PR or Season Best using the buttons on the top.

  4. 4

    Watch the Race

    At this point your teams have been created and it's time to watch the race. Cheer on all of your teammates and keep an eye out for your FantasyXC runners!

  5. 5

    Enter Results

    Once the race is over, enter each of their individual race places. Once completed, the winner of the draft will be revealed and crowned as a FantasyXC Champion!
